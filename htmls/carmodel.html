This is one of my main hobbies I do which is to model fictional cars in a game called automation the car company tycoon.

What is Automation?

Well it's a game on steam where you pretty much can make any sort of car you want, be it a modern italian supercar, classic V8 american muscle, or your everyday automobile you find on the road. It pretty much is blender lite in some of the aspects in how you can use 3D and get the required car you want.

I got automation about a year ago and I still from time to time model cars moreso for challenges from either the developers of the game or mainly through the community forums.
